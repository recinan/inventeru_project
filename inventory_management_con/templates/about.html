InventERU

About Us

InventERU is an innovative SaaS platform that digitalizes warehouse management, simplifying stock tracking and optimizing business processes. With core features such as adding warehouses, managing categories, and tracking products, InventERU offers great flexibility to your business, providing full control over inventory management.

Why Choose InventERU?

Every business has unique warehouse management needs. At InventERU, we provide powerful tools that can be customized to fit the specific needs of your business. You can add warehouses, create custom categories for each warehouse, and easily track products within those categories. Additionally, generating reports on products in your warehouses is seamless, ensuring that you always have the correct information at your fingertips.

Our Features

  • Add and Manage Warehouses: Add multiple warehouses and easily track stock levels and settings for each warehouse.
  • Category and Product Management: Within each warehouse, you can create categories to group your products, keeping everything organized and accessible.
  • Real-Time Product Tracking: Monitor your products' inventory status in real-time and track any changes instantly.
  • Output Generation: Generate reports on products in your warehouses, making it easier to assess stock levels and perform analysis.
  • Cloud-Based Access: With our cloud-based platform, you can access your system from anywhere, on any device.
